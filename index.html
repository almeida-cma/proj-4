B-Roll (30-45s)

city traffic bus stop urban mobility crowded bus subway station bike lane traffic jam commuting people

Dados (15-20s)

Buscar gráficos:

bar chart data visualization infographic

  • 📊 78% consideram qualidade ruim
  • 📊 52% gastam +2h por dia

Trilha Sonora

Buscar áudios:

corporate background documentary light news ambient

Configurações:

🔊 Volume: 20-30%

🎚️ Fade in / Fade out

🎯 Neutra, não emocional

Encerramento

Buscar vídeos:

city skyline sunset news outro background closing news
